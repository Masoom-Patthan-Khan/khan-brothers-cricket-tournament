Tournament Rules & Regulations

Complete guidelines for Khan Brothers Cricket Tournament 2026

üéÆ Tournament Format

Match Structure

  • T20 format - 20 overs per innings
  • League stage followed by knockout rounds
  • Semi-finals and finals for top teams
  • All matches will be played with tennis ball
  • White clothing preferred but not mandatory

Overs & Bowling

  • Each bowler can bowl maximum 4 overs
  • Powerplay: First 6 overs (maximum 2 fielders outside 30-yard circle)
  • No-balls and wides: 1 extra run + 1 extra ball
  • Free hit for front-foot no-balls

Match Timing

  • Toss will be conducted 15 minutes before scheduled time
  • Teams must report 30 minutes before match time
  • Grace period: 15 minutes (beyond that = walkover)
  • Match abandoned if rain/bad light occurs

üë• Team Composition & Registration

Team Size

  • Minimum 11 players required
  • Maximum 15 players allowed per team
  • 11 players on field at a time
  • Substitutes allowed only for injury (fielding only)

Team Registration

  • Team name must be unique and appropriate
  • All players must be registered before first match
  • No player addition after tournament starts
  • Player cannot represent more than one team
  • Team captain must attend pre-tournament meeting

Team Uniform

  • All team members should wear similar colored jerseys
  • Jersey numbers recommended
  • Cricket shoes or sports shoes mandatory
  • Protective gear recommended (pads, helmet, gloves)

‚úÖ Player Eligibility Criteria

Age Requirements

  • Minimum age: 16 years (as on January 1, 2025)
  • No upper age limit
  • Age proof mandatory (Aadhar card, school certificate, etc.)

Documentation Required

  • Valid government ID proof (Aadhar/Driving License/Voter ID)
  • Two passport-size photographs
  • Age proof certificate
  • Player registration form (provided by organizers)

Professional Players

  • Players who have played Ranji Trophy, IPL, or international cricket are NOT eligible
  • Players from district/state teams must declare their status
  • Organizers reserve right to verify and disqualify

Local Community Rule

  • At least 7 players must be from local community
  • Guest players allowed (maximum 4 per team)
  • Proof of residence may be required

Player Conduct

  • Respect umpires, opponents, and spectators
  • No abusive language or obscene gestures
  • Accept umpire decisions gracefully
  • Shake hands before and after the match
  • Follow spirit of cricket at all times

Strictly Prohibited

  • Smoking or alcohol consumption on ground
  • Betting or match-fixing (lifetime ban)
  • Damaging tournament property
  • Threatening or intimidating behavior

Penalties

  • Yellow card: Warning for minor misconduct
  • Red card: Immediate removal from match
  • Team disqualification for serious violations
  • Prize money forfeiture for rule violations
  • Ban from future tournaments (for severe cases)
